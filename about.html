About ShivKumbh

Empowering students across India with quality education since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a group of IIT alumni, ShivKumbh began with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to every student in India.

What started as a small coaching initiative in Delhi has now grown into India's leading online learning platform, serving over 10 million students across the country. Our innovative teaching methods and personalized learning approach have helped countless students achieve academic excellence.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do

Our Mission

To democratize quality education by making it accessible, affordable, and engaging for students across India, regardless of their geographical location or economic background.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted learning platform that transforms how students learn by leveraging technology and innovative pedagogy to create future-ready individuals.

Our Core Values

The foundation of our educational philosophy

Student First

Every decision we make is guided by what's best for our students' learning and growth.

Innovation

We constantly evolve our teaching methods to make learning more effective and engaging.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty and transparency in all our interactions.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork between students, parents, and educators.

Excellence

Accessibility
